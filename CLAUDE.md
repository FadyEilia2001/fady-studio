# CLAUDE.md

## Design Context

This is **fady-studio** — a bilingual (EN/AR, RTL-aware) personal studio/portfolio for Fady Eilia, who builds custom apps & websites, automation tooling, and brand work for small/medium businesses.

- **Strategy & register:** see [PRODUCT.md](PRODUCT.md). Register is **brand** (the design IS the product).
- **Visual system:** see [DESIGN.md](DESIGN.md) for tokens, type scale, and components.

**The 5 design principles (from PRODUCT.md):**
1. Memorable beats tidy.
2. Bold execution, human voice.
3. Show the work works (audience is non-technical, skeptical SMB owners).
4. Not-a-studio-template (reject the saturated "creative studio" / SaaS grammar).
5. Two languages, one quality bar (Arabic/RTL is first-class, not a mirror afterthought).

Accessibility floor: WCAG 2.2 AA + full `prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives + RTL parity.
